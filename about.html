About us

Littel Caterpillers

A place where protected little chrysalides are incubated to grow into beautiful, sensible and independent butterflies that spread the joy of freedom and beauty of life.

Little Caterpillars is gradually gaining remarkable growth after its launch in the year 2017. The ambitions and aspirations behind launching the school are the driving force towards the attainment of the motive in a systematic and disciplined manner. Little Caterpillars aims at providing a holistic learning environment that enables the children to adapt to the changing environmental factors.

Philosophy

Our Philosophy

Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all.

Education does not just start or end with the alphabets or numbers. Destined ends do not just depend on these alphanumeric characters. We need to understand the language of the little hearts and never-ending question mark minds that look forward to their parents and teachers for answers. The children need a kind of environment, where their minds are free to shoot out their unending queries and provides free spacious play areas where they satisfy their quench for playing. Little Caterpillars was started with a motive to achieve these goals. As researches suggest, the primary years of the children are crucial for the development of their body and mind. Little Caterpillars assures to give complete assistance to the parents who rely on it for the holistic development of their children. It provides its joiners with the best learning methodologies, infra structural facilities and technical advancements in the field of pre-school learning. Its huge play area is one dream option for any child to play.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission and Vision

we aim to provide each child with a nurturing and stimulating environment that promotes their overall growth and development

Our Mission

To nurture and fortify every little mind in a holistic learning environment, inculcating the ethical, cultural and social values that help them adapt to the demands of contemporary society.

Our Vision

Incessantly endeavouring to realize our vision by providing the sophisticated learning resources and applying the technological advancements in educating our future generations.

Team

Our Team
